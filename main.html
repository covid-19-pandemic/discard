about this project

   Do things look a little incomplete around here? There's a reason for that--this website is a part of Charlotte Chen's senior "DIY" project. This means that the focus was to learn more about the creation process and inner workings of html games and to learn more about the mechanisms of SARS-CoV-2 rather than create a fully functioning website.

   Even though it's not the greatest thing ever and it's not quite the most creative, it was one that was immensely rewarding. The information I learned from researching is invaluable. There's nothing quite like the instant gratification of html, css, and js. Although these aren't the most difficult languages to learn, much of the websites we see and use are constructed with html. Understanding a bit more about the inner workings helps me to fully appreciate the time and dedication that likely went into the highly interactive and complex websites we have available to us.
